Area of specialization

Dr. Fedire's practice specializes in unicompartmental knee surgery, which he has been performing for decade now, and more notably involving Oxford knee replacements for medial compartment osteoarthritis. As well as, arthroscopic surgeries in the hip, knee and shoulder, and minimally invasive techniques including partial knee replacements. On occasion, an interpositional Orthoglide arthroplasty can be performed, usually for lateral compartment osteoarthritis.

Dr. Fedire obtained his medical degree in 1991 from School Of Medicine. He became a specialist in orthopedic surgery in 1997, after a five year orthopedic training program at the school of Medicine. Since September of 1997, Dr. Fedire has been practicing general orthopedic surgery in White Rock, B.C.

Dr. Fedire is a member of the British Columbia Medical Association, Canadian Medical Association, Canadian Orthopedic Association, British Columbia Orthopedic Association, American Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons, Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada, and Society of Specialist Physicians and Surgeons of B.C.
